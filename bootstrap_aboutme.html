About Me

My name is Sadie Byun. As well as being a senior at The King's Academy, I am a passionate lover of all furry creatures, an intense hater of tomatoes, an inspired traveler, and a student programmer!

In the summer of 2016, I had the amazing opportunity to learn basic computer programming at the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. In the course of 7 weeks, I learned the basics in Scratch, Python, C++, HTML, CSS, and Javascript. With what I've learned, I've created this page to share with you who I am.

One day, I aspire to use my life experiences and programming knowledge to help at-risk children and women in underdeveloped Latin American countries get the attention, necessities, and love they need to be empowered and impactful forces in their communities.
